Welcome to the webpage of the workshop "Dynamical and Quantum Systems"

The aim of this workshop is to gather a small group of people working on dynamical and quantum systems. This will be the occasion to celebrate the 60th birthday of Eric Séré.

If you would like to attend the workshop, please register by contacting the organizers.
